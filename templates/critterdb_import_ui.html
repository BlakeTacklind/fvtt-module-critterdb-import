To get your critter or bestiary from CritterDB:
  • For a single creature click on the down arrow in the upper right to expand, then select "Export", "Export to JSON", "CritterDB JSON", and copy to clipboard
  • For an entire bestiary, open it and click the three-dots menu in the upper right, then select "Export", "CritterDB JSON", and copy to clipboard
Paste the JSON into the box below, select whether you want to overwrite any existing compendia, and click "Import".
